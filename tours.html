Tours

Come experience Magma Stills

We would like to invite you to join us for a complimentary tour every Saturday from 1pm to 3pm at our Lava Fields location. Our tours offer a comprehensive and immersive experience, starting with a guided walk through the beautiful distillery grounds, followed by a visit to our state-of-the-art distillery, and then on to our impressive barrel warehouse. The final stop on our tour is our inviting tasting lounge, where you can sample some of our finest spirits. We look forward to sharing our passion for handcrafted spirits with you in a fun and informative setting.

Explore Our History

During our tour, we offer a unique opportunity to delve into the rich history of Magma Stills and learn about the fascinating family background of the 3 mates who started it all. Our in-depth exploration takes you on a journey through our annals, providing insight into our past, present, and future. We are proud to use our heritage as a source of inspiration to push the boundaries of the craft of distilling, and we are excited to share this story with you. Join us for an unforgettable experience where you can discover the secrets behind our exceptional spirits and the people who made it all possible.

Join Us For A Tasting

Our tour concludes in our inviting tasting lounge where you can savor the unique flavors of all of Magma Stills' exceptional spirits. It's the perfect opportunity to relax and enjoy a moment of indulgence after an informative and fascinating journey through our distillery. We invite you to join us and discover the taste of our handcrafted spirits that are a result of the passion and dedication of our team.
