What is Magma Stills

Magma Stills is a New Zealand based Distillery, that is dedicated to bringing high quality liquor to the land of the long white cloud.

Using geothermal energy from underground thermal vents, we ferment our alcohol in the greenest way possible with zero emissions and zero waste. Here at Magma Stills we believe in the art of recycling and reusing whatever we can, that's why we make sure to melt and recast all broken bottles.

It's our promise to you, that we can make the cleanest and meanest liquor in NZ

Magma Stills Gin, crafted with passion, made with skill, drunk with everything.

To create is to care, and care is exactly what we prioritise when it comes to making our liquor.
